Level requirement 31
Best taming food is kibble. Other taming food from best to worst: Botany bale bait, veggie bag bait, berry bag bait. Baits can be crafted at the bait station.
Put taming food into your last quickslot (0) and use it to drop it on the ground near the creature. After a while you can feed the creature from hand.
Occasionally the creature wants you to pet them. The reaction changes depending on the taming %
0 - 25% - the creature is startled and may run away
25% and above - the creature enjoys petting
55%  - the creature will want to be cared every now and again
